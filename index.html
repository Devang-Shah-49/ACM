_FAQs_

How big can my team be?

The maximum team size allowed is 4.

Can I come individually?

No. A team of minimum 2 members is allowed.

When?

CODEBASH 2021 will be a 24 hours hackathon which will stretch from 27th October to 28th October 2021.

Where?

Online due to Covid Protocols.

How much does it cost?

CODEBASH is free of cost.

What are the criteria of shortlisting the teams?

The registered participants will be shortlisted based on their DevFolio Profiles.

What is the eligibility criteria?

You must be a student from any University/College or Higher Secondary School in India.
